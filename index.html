ROLLING ADVERTISEMENT FOR RECRUITMENT TO THE POST OF SENIOR RESIDENTS (NON-ACADEMIC) PURELY ON TEMPORARY BASIS AT AIIMS BHOPAL
All India Institute of Medical Sciences, Bhopal an Institute of National Importance (INI) invites online applications from Indian Nationals for the post of Senior Residents (Non-Academic) under Govt. of India, Residency Scheme, 1992 and or as amended from time to time. The aspiring candidates satisfying the eligibility criteria in all respect can submit their application through ONLINE mode only. The online registration of application is made available on AIIMS, Bhopal official website, i.e. http://www.aiimsbhopal.edu.in.
Advertisement for the post of Junior Research fellow (for ICMR funded Extramural project)
Online application share invited from eligible candidates to attend Interview on 02.08.2023for the post of Junior Research Fellow(JRF) on purely contractual basis in the extramural research project titled “HTA of national Stroke Care Registry Program: Development of Hospital Based Stroke Registries (HSBR) in different regions of India” sanctioned to Dr. Priyanka V Kashyap, Associate Professor in the Department of Neurology at AIIMS Bhopal, as Principal Investigator under Ad-hoc-Scheme of the Indian Council of medical research(ICMR)India.
